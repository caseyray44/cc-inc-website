Don't Let Ice Destroy Your Roof

Serving Crosslake, Nisswa, Brainerd, and the entire Whitefish Chain.

Minnesota winters are tough on cabins. When heavy snow piles up and temperatures fluctuate, Ice Dams form at your roof's edge, forcing water to back up under your shingles and leak into your walls. In the Brainerd Lakes Area and Crosslake, this is the #1 cause of winter water damage.

The CC Inc Safety Difference: Steam, Not Hammers.

Many "handymen" try to remove ice dams with axes, hammers, or pressure washers. This destroys your roof.

We use professional-grade low-pressure steam. This gently melts the ice away from the shingles without any impact or abrasion. It is the only method recommended by roofing manufacturers.
